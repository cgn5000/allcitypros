Frequently Asked Questions

How much does lawn care cost in Santa Ana?
Weekly mowing and edging in Santa Ana runs $30–$80 per visit depending on yard size. Monthly lawn care packages average $150–$400. Full landscape design and installation projects are priced per scope — most Santa Ana landscapers offer free estimates.
How often should my lawn be mowed in Santa Ana?
During peak growing season, most Santa Ana lawns need mowing every 7–10 days. Cool-season grasses may slow in summer heat; warm-season grasses grow fastest in summer. Your landscaper will recommend a frequency that keeps your lawn healthy without over-cutting.
What's the best time to plant in Santa Ana?
Planting timing depends on your plant type and CA's climate zone. Generally, spring (after last frost) and fall (6 weeks before first frost) are ideal for most plants. A Santa Ana landscape designer can recommend plants suited to your specific soil and sun conditions.
How do I get rid of weeds without hurting my lawn?
Pre-emergent herbicides applied in early spring prevent weed seeds from germinating. Post-emergent products target existing weeds. Thick, healthy turf is your best long-term defense — proper fertilization, watering, and mowing height all help. A Santa Ana lawn care professional can develop a year-round program.
Do I need an irrigation system in Santa Ana?
Irrigation systems pay for themselves in water savings and plant health, especially during Santa Ana's dry seasons. A smart drip or sprinkler system installed by a Santa Ana landscaper typically costs $2,500–$6,000 and can reduce outdoor water use by 30–50%.
