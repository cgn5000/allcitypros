About Orlando Lending Pros

Orlando Lending Pros helps Orlando, FL homebuyers and homeowners navigate the mortgage process with confidence. Our licensed mortgage advisors shop rates across dozens of lenders to find you the best possible rate and terms — whether you're purchasing your first home, moving up, refinancing for a lower payment, or tapping home equity.

We work with conventional, FHA, VA, USDA, and jumbo loan programs, and our team is experienced with first-time buyer grants and down payment assistance programs available in FL. From pre-approval to closing, we handle the paperwork and keep you informed at every milestone so there are no surprises at the settlement table.

Orlando clients choose us for our speed — most pre-approvals within 24 hours — and for our commitment to finding the right loan, not just closing quickly. We're independently licensed, which means our loyalty is to you, not to a single lender. Get your free rate quote today.
