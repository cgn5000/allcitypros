About ZenPet Raleigh

ZenPet Raleigh provides compassionate, professional pet care services for dogs, cats, and small animals throughout Raleigh, NC. Whether your pet needs a full grooming session, boarding while you travel, daycare during the workday, or obedience training, our experienced team treats every animal like family.

Our grooming services include breed-appropriate cuts, baths, nail trims, ear cleaning, and teeth brushing — performed in a calm, stress-free environment. Boarding guests stay in spacious, climate-controlled suites with daily exercise, playtime, and individual attention. Our certified trainers offer private lessons, group classes, and puppy socialization programs backed by positive reinforcement methods.

Raleigh pet owners love us for our transparent communication — daily updates and photos during boarding and daycare — and for the genuine love our staff shows every animal. All facilities are clean, safe, and fully insured. Book your pet's first visit today.
