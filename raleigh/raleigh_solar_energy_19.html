About SolarFirst Raleigh

SolarFirst Raleigh helps Raleigh, NC homeowners and businesses reduce their energy bills and carbon footprint with professionally installed solar panel systems. Our certified solar installers handle everything from roof assessment and system design through permitting, installation, grid interconnection, and utility incentive filing.

We work with top-tier panel manufacturers — LG, SunPower, Panasonic, and REC — and offer multiple battery storage solutions including the Tesla Powerwall and Enphase IQ Battery, so you stay powered even during grid outages. Our team identifies every available federal, state, and utility incentive, including the 30% federal tax credit, to maximize your savings from day one.

The average Raleigh homeowner who goes solar saves thousands over the life of their system. We back all installations with a 25-year panel warranty and a 10-year workmanship guarantee. Get a free solar assessment and savings estimate today — no pressure, no obligation.
