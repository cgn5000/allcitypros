About EliteFit Miami

EliteFit Miami helps Miami, FL residents achieve their fitness goals through personalized, science-backed training programs. Our certified personal trainers design every workout plan around your specific body, goals, and lifestyle — whether you want to lose weight, build muscle, improve athletic performance, or simply feel better every day.

We offer one-on-one personal training, small group sessions, and nutrition coaching in private studio settings, at your home gym, or outdoors. Every program starts with a comprehensive fitness assessment so we understand your current level, history, and any injuries or limitations. From there we build a progressive plan and adjust it as you improve.

Miami clients choose us because we get results — and because we hold you accountable with the right mix of encouragement and challenge. Our trainers are certified through NASM, ACE, or NSCA and carry full liability insurance. Book a free intro session today.
