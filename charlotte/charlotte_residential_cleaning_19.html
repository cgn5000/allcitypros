Frequently Asked Questions

How much does house cleaning cost in Charlotte?
Professional house cleaning in Charlotte typically costs $100–$200 for a standard clean of a 1,500 sq ft home. Deep cleans, move-out cleans, and post-construction cleans run higher — usually $200–$400+. Most services offer free instant quotes online.
How often should I have my home professionally cleaned?
Most homeowners schedule professional cleaning every 2–4 weeks. Weekly service suits busy households or homes with pets and kids; bi-weekly is the most popular frequency; monthly works well for single occupants or low-traffic homes.
Are cleaning products safe for kids and pets?
Reputable Charlotte cleaning companies use EPA-approved, non-toxic products that are safe for children and pets. Ask your cleaning service about their product line — many offer fully green cleaning options at no extra charge.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
No — most Charlotte cleaning clients provide a key or door code and aren't home during service. All professional cleaning staff are background-checked and insured, so you can feel comfortable leaving them access to your home.
What's included in a deep clean vs a regular clean?
A regular clean covers standard surfaces, floors, bathrooms, and kitchen. A deep clean adds inside appliances, baseboards, window sills, ceiling fans, grout scrubbing, and other detail work. Most Charlotte cleaning companies recommend starting with a deep clean, then switching to maintenance visits.
