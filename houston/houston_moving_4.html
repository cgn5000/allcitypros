About Houston Van Lines

Houston Van Lines makes relocation stress-free for families and businesses throughout Houston, TX. Whether you're moving across town or across the country, our trained crews handle every step — packing, loading, transport, unloading, and furniture placement — with the care your belongings deserve.

We use professional-grade packing materials and furniture protection on every move, and our GPS-tracked fleet means you always know where your possessions are. Specialty items like pianos, artwork, and antiques get dedicated wrapping and handling procedures. We also offer short- and long-term storage solutions at our climate-controlled facility.

Thousands of Houston families have trusted us with their most important moves. We're fully licensed, insured, and registered with the FMCSA. Get a free in-home or virtual estimate today — no hidden fees, no surprises on moving day.
