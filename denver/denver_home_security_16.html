Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a home security system cost in Denver?
Professional security systems in Denver typically cost $200–$800 for equipment, plus $20–$60/month for 24/7 monitoring. Smart home integration (cameras, smart locks, video doorbell) adds to equipment cost. Many companies offer $0-down equipment with a monitoring contract.
Do I need a professional installer or can I DIY?
DIY systems (Ring, SimpliSafe, Wyze) are cheaper and flexible but lack professional installation and often have higher false-alarm rates. Professional Denver security companies handle installation, system programming, and integrate all devices for maximum reliability.
What deters burglars most effectively?
Studies show visible security cameras, alarm system signage, motion-activated lighting, and reinforced door locks are the most effective deterrents. A Denver security professional can assess your home's vulnerabilities and recommend a layered approach.
How fast does a monitoring center respond in Denver?
Professional monitoring centers have average response times of 30–45 seconds. When an alarm triggers, they attempt to contact you, then dispatch police, fire, or medical services as needed. Local Denver response times after dispatch depend on your area's emergency services.
Does a security system lower my homeowner's insurance in Denver?
Yes — most CO insurance carriers offer 5–20% discounts for professionally monitored security systems. Discounts vary by carrier and system type. Ask your Denver security company to provide documentation for your insurance agent.
