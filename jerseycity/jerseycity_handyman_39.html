Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a handyman cost in Jersey City?
Handyman rates in Jersey City typically run $60–$120/hr, with most companies having a 2-hour minimum. Flat-rate pricing is common for defined jobs (e.g., TV mounting $100–$150, ceiling fan install $75–$125, drywall patch $100–$250). Always get a written estimate before work starts.
What jobs does a handyman do vs a licensed contractor?
Handymen handle general repairs, installations, and maintenance that don't require a specialty license — drywall, caulking, door hardware, furniture assembly, minor carpentry. Jobs involving structural changes, electrical panels, plumbing lines, or gas require licensed Jersey City contractors with permits.
How do I find a reliable handyman in Jersey City?
Ask neighbors for referrals, check Google and Nextdoor reviews, verify they're insured (ask for a certificate), and get a written scope and estimate before any work begins. Reliable Jersey City handymen are booked out 1–2 weeks — if someone is immediately available at a very low price, be cautious.
Can a handyman do multiple small jobs in one visit?
Yes — this is one of a handyman's biggest advantages over specialty contractors. Most Jersey City handymen will work through your honey-do list in a single visit, making efficient use of the minimum charge. Group your small jobs together to maximize value.
Is a handyman insured in Jersey City?
Always ask for proof of general liability insurance before letting anyone into your home. Professional Jersey City handyman services carry at least $1M in liability coverage. This protects you if they accidentally damage your property or cause an injury on site.
