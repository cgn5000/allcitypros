Frequently Asked Questions

How much does pool maintenance cost in Louisville?
Weekly pool service in Louisville typically costs $100–$200/month for chemical balancing, cleaning, and equipment checks. Equipment repairs are billed separately. Many Louisville pool companies offer bundled service plans that include chemical costs and a set number of repairs per year.
How often should my pool be serviced?
During swim season, weekly service is standard. Off-season, every 2 weeks is often sufficient. Pools left unserviced quickly develop algae, imbalanced chemistry, and equipment wear. A regular Louisville pool service plan is the most cost-effective way to protect your investment.
How do I close my pool for winter in Louisville?
Pool winterization in Louisville involves balancing chemicals, lowering the water level, blowing out lines, adding antifreeze (in freeze-risk areas), covering the pool, and winterizing the pump and filter. Most Louisville pool companies offer seasonal closing packages starting at $150–$300.
How long does pool construction take in Louisville?
A typical in-ground pool build in Louisville takes 6–12 weeks from excavation to final inspection, depending on design complexity, material availability, and permit timelines. Your Louisville pool builder should provide a detailed timeline with milestones before construction begins.
What chemicals do I need to maintain my pool?
Core pool chemicals include chlorine (sanitizer), pH increaser/decreaser, alkalinity increaser, calcium hardness increaser, and algaecide. Target ranges: pH 7.2–7.6, chlorine 1–3 ppm, alkalinity 80–120 ppm. A Louisville pool service tech will handle all of this on your weekly service visits.
